Overview

What It Does

Live in-game ‚Äî class colors,
zones & saved status visible

Channel
KnightGuard is the dedicated channel. Hit Join on a fresh character and the window opens, joins, sets chat color, and refreshes ‚Äî all automatically.
Class Color
Each name is colored by class, learned from your guild roster and friends list. White means unknown class ‚Äî it builds up as more guildmates appear.
Zone
Small grey text below each name shows current location. Refreshed every time you open the window. Requires them to be on your friends list or in your guild.
Online Status
Green dot ‚Äî currently in the channel. Grey dot ‚Äî saved to your list but not currently visible in channel.
Saved List
[saved] players persist between sessions and logins. [+save] means they're in channel right now but not yet on your list.
Chat Color
KnightGuard messages display in light blue automatically. Reapplied after every zone change since 1.12 resets chat colours on transitions.

  2. 2 Launch Turtle WoW. At the character select screen click AddOns and verify ChannelFriends ‚úì is checked and enabled.
